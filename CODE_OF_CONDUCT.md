# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

By participating, you agree to uphold this code. Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by [opening a private issue](https://github.com/ferrumclaudepilgrim/claude-code-android/issues/new) marked "[CONDUCT]" in the title, or by contacting the maintainer through their [GitHub profile](https://github.com/FerrumFluxFenice).
